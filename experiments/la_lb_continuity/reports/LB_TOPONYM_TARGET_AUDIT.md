## Honest handling of the five known-pair LB forms (DEVELOPMENT only)
`pa-i-to`, `tu-ri-so`, `se-to-i-ja` are attested bare forms. **`di-ka-ta` and `i-da` are NOT attested
as bare forms in DĀMOS** — only the derived `di-ka-ta-de`/`di-ka-ta-jo` (Diktaian) and `i-da-i-jo`
(Idaian). We therefore record the *attested derived forms* with a note; the identifications for
`i-da`(-i-jo) (conf 0.55) and `se-to-i-ja` (0.70) are explicitly debated. All five are **DEVELOPMENT**
(debug/positive-control), `included_in_known_five=true`; they are **not** generalization evidence.

## Confidence discipline
`identification_confidence` is per-target and honest: secure (Phaistos 0.95, Knossos 0.95, Amnisos
0.90, Kydonia 0.90, Lyktos 0.85) down to uncertain (`qa-ra` 0.45, `ra-su-to`/`ti-ri-to`/`ri-jo-no`/
`u-ta-no`/`ra-ja`/`da-*22-to` 0.50). Low-confidence targets are retained but flagged; they feed the
sensitivity tier, not a headline result. `da-*22-to` carries an undeciphered sign (`*22`) in its raw
sequence, preserved as-is.
