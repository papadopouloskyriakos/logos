# GENRE_MISMATCH_AUDIT — §VIII

_The single most important interpretive fact about this pass._

## The mismatch
- The **primary frozen channel is administrative**: PRIMARY candidates are internally-selected
  administrative `TOPONYM_LIKE` Linear A forms (entry-leading, recurrent across the accounting network).
- **Most previously published LA↔LB continuities are ritual or localized**: of the five known pairs,
  only PA-I-TO is administrative; TU-RU-SA, I-DA, SE-TO-I-JA are attested on **stone vessels (the
  libation/ritual register)**, and DI-KI-TA is absent from silver as the bare form (§VI).
- **The five known forms are therefore not representative of the primary candidate universe.** Their
  `0/5` overlap with PRIMARY/SENSITIVITY is a **domain mismatch**, not a positive-control failure.

## Consequence
The primary test evaluates a **distinct hypothesis** — *administrative* toponym continuity — from the
one the famous examples illustrate (largely *ritual* continuity). This audit concludes **neither**:
- that ritual continuities are invalid (they are out of this pass's scope, not refuted); **nor**
- that the administrative channel is validated (it returned 0 with, at best, exact-only power).
