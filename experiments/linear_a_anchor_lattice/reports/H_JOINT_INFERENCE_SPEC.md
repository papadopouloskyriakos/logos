## 3. The three solvers

- **H_MODEL_1 — Bayesian factor graph, Gibbs.** Variables: sign values over D. Factors: one
  collapsed anchor factor per pinned sign (mixture weight ρ), calibrated rel-pair factors (LLR
  from §2), negligible-weight bridge/stroke terms (=excluded at weight 0). 3 chains × 3000
  sweeps, burn-in 1000, seed 20260708. Output: per-sign marginal entropy per scenario. A
  naive-stacked variant (anchor factor ^n_clone_edges) is run once to quantify the Art.-XI error.
- **H_MODEL_2 — CP / exact counting.** Hard-constraint views: arc-consistent per-sign domains,
  UNSAT/violation audit of hard-anchors+hard-rel (are the two channels mutually consistent?),
  backbone (forced) signs, exact model counts by variable elimination on small components +
  sequential-importance-sampling counts (20k particles) for large ones; gauge quotient by
  G = Sym(12 non-∅ C) × Sym(5 V) (relabeling collapse) for unpinned components.
- **H_MODEL_3 — MDL / penalized beam search.** Flags (channel validity: L, REL; 1 bit each) ×
  assignments; assignment cost log2(67)/sign; data credit per explained pin-slot/edge. Three
  accounting levels: naive_raw (47 clone edges counted), dedup (unique sign-pins), and
  art12_discounted (pins generated by the graded rule earn 0 — Art. VIII/XII effective_n).
  Beam K=500, tie window Δ=1 bit; tied systems collapsed by relabeling signature
  (same-C/same-V partition structure + absolute pins).

## 4. Null + sensitivity + agreement

- **Random-anchor null** (N=200, seed 20260708): permute which signs the 47 value-bearing edges
  attach to (uniform over the 163-sign universe, arity preserved); recompute per-sign entropy
  reduction analytically at S2; compare the real A-only mean reduction against the null
  distribution (one-sided: is real BELOW null?).
- **Sensitivity**: rerun pins/rel extraction + M1-analytic + M2 domains under conservative (163)
  / split (166) / merged (158) universes via the allograph maps.
- **Agreement**: per-sign "constrained" sets from the three solvers at matched scenario;
  Jaccard + sign-level table.
