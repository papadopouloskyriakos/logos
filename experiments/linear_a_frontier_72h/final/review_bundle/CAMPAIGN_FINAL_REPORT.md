## Executive summary

The campaign ran **105 pre-registered epochs** across **34 method families** (F1–F23 + campaign-wide), each with a
committed prediction, controls, an adaptive null, and a mechanical verdict computed from held-out corpus data — never
by the proposer LLM. It was designed to find a *real* Linear A reading and to refuse a *fitted* one.

It refused a fitted one. **Zero transfer licences were earned; the highest claim layer reached is L3 (functional-
structural); no L4+ (semantic / phonetic / language-ID / translation) claim is authorized.** The absolute-value gate
(E102) fails on every criterion because the campaign's apparent multi-method signal collapses, under a dependency-
adjusted independence audit, to a **single independent channel** (context co-occurrence).

What the campaign *did* produce is durable and honest:

- **One graduated relative finding — A- prefixation (E022/E023/E024/E103).** A- is a productive, corpus-wide,
  word-initial positional **prefix SLOT** in Linear A administrative words. It survives a matched adaptive null
  (p=.0002, best-of-72 maxT, E022), cross-site held-out (significant in 9/10 sites + leave-one-site-out, E023),
  multi-axis held-out (5/6 support types + 4/4 chronological phases, E024), and — the closure adjudication —
  **all three independent segmentation schemes** (editor / divider-strict / numeral-anchored ledger-heads; A- is
  rank-1 by best-of-universe maxT with z≈17, ~2.4× the next sign, E103). It is a **RELATIVE positional constraint
  (L2/L3), NOT a phonetic value.**
- **A campaign-wide null certifying the discipline (E104):** running the *actual* graduation machinery on
  structure-matched, signal-free nulls fabricates a graduated finding **0.5%** of the time (CP95 upper 2.35%) — vs
  **30.5%** with the best-of-universe maxT deflation ablated. The multiplicity gate is load-bearing (60× false-
  positive suppression) and power is retained (planted prefix recovered 40/40). A-'s z≈17 sits far outside the null.
- **A method-diverse re-confirmation of underdetermination (F11 morphogenesis + F12 cross-disciplinary, E091–E102):**
  two whole disciplines of imported machinery (graph reaction-diffusion with mechanically-verified Turing
  instability; error-correcting codes / belief propagation; Potts spin-glass; causal source separation; topological
  persistence; global network-flow) calibrate on blinded Linear B but deliver **no Linear A decipherment**. The one
  genuine linguistic signal they surface (E099: the weak Linear B vowel structure is frequency-*invariant*, not a
  frequency artifact) is **single-channel and LA-untestable**.
