## Explicitly resolved / quarantined ambiguities
- **ARKH site ambiguity:** silver is internally consistent (`Arkhalkhori` = Arkalochori, 14 docs);
  SigLA labels the same docs `Arkhanes`. Cross-source disagreement **flagged** (`site_ambiguity`) on
  the 3 affected candidates; it does **not** change the silver-internal `n_sites`/tier, so tiers are
  stable. Not silently merged.
- **Site aliases/typos:** `Malia→Mallia`, `Haghios Stehanos→Haghios Stephanos` canonicalized in
  `site_canonical`; silver has one spelling per site internally, so no candidate's recurrence is
  double-counted.
- **Face/join & uncertain docs:** candidates whose document set includes `(?)`, `+`, `<`, `>` markers
  are flagged (`uncertainty.join_or_uncertain_doc`).
- **SigLA-composite vs silver-decomposition:** candidates whose raw IDs include an A-series/unmapped
  sign (where the two sources transcribe differently) are flagged `uncertainty.composite_sensitive`
  (3 candidates) — carried to the §XII transcription-variant sensitivity, not resolved now.
- **Duplicate / non-independent attestation:** candidates recurring across multiple docs at a **single
  site** are flagged `single_site_possible_nonindependence` (formulaic-repeat risk). Distinct
  word-forms are already deduped (one record per form).
- **Chronology:** each candidate carries its silver `chronology` (period set); many LA administrative
  forms are `LMIB`. Cross-horizon comparison to LB (LM II–III) is inherent and carried to §XIV.
