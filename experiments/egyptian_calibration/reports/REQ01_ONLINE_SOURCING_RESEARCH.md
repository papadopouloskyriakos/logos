# REQ-01 — Online sourcing of the Kom el-Hetan primary edition (2026-07-06)

Task: locate and download the **primary edition** of the Amenhotep III Kom el-Hetan Aegean
toponym list — **Edel & Görg 2005** *(Die Ortsnamenlisten im nördlichen Säulenhof des
Totentempels Amenophis' III.)* or **Kitchen's full topographical-list collation** — so future
Cretan toponym anchors can move from `CONFIRMATORY_INELIGIBLE` toward a freezable target set.

## What is actually obtainable online (all legitimately)

- **Kitchen 2009** — free PDF mirrored at Memphis
  (`blogs.memphis.edu/murnane/files/2014/03/Kitchen-12bzvto.pdf`). Downloaded and verified
  **byte-identical** to the copy already in hand: `sha256 b1a6c8fa7f66446e…`. It is an 8-page
  *discussion* (List E is described in prose — "Knossos, Lyktos, Amnisos, Phaistos in Crete;
  … Kythera, … Nauplia, Mycenae, Messenia, Cydonia, plus Wilios"), with **no** row-by-row
  hieroglyphic collation. **"Kitchen's full collation" does not exist as a free download.**
- **Cline & Stannish 2011** — "Sailing the Great Green Sea? Amenhotep III's 'Aegean List' from
  Kom el-Hetan, Once More," *JAEI* 3.2, 6–16. Open PDF (ancientportsantiques.com; academia.edu).
  Already in hand (secondary). Gives the core readings in prose: Knossos `kA-n-yw-SA`, second
  Amnisos `i-m-n-y-SA`, Lyktos `ry-kA-ti`, and the Edel & Görg reconstruction of Phaistos /
  Kydonia in the two final ovals. **Secondary re-edition, not the primary edition.**
- **Bělohoubková 2014** — Dana Bělohoubková, BA thesis, Charles University Prague / Czech
  Institute of Egyptology (supervisor J. Mynářová): *"Aegean list" from the temple of Amenhotep
  III at Kom el-Hettân: mapping the current stage of knowledge.* 73 pp; Edel cited 51×; image
  appendix (plates) pp. 53–67; comparative appendix table ~p. 70. **Delivered manually by the
  user** (scp → this host, 2026-07-06); ingested to gitignored bronze, `sha256 91a0035bdfb4a7a4…`.
  A **secondary compilation** that tabulates the readings and the editorial apparatus — see the
  companion determination report for what it does/does not resolve.
- **German Wikipedia**, *"Liste ägäischer Ortsnamen vom Totentempel Amenophis III."* — a
  faithful tertiary reproduction of the Edel & Görg 2005 row-by-row list (used only as an
  independent cross-check of the readings, never as an authority).
- **Simons 1937** *(Handbook for the Study of Egyptian Topographical Lists Relating to Western
  Asia)* — the foundational methodological handbook, but it covers **Western Asia, not the
  Aegean list**; not relevant to the En target set.

## Determination

**REQ-01 cannot be closed by free download.** Neither the primary edition (Edel & Görg 2005)
nor its *editio princeps* (Edel 1966) is available online; the only free "Kitchen" item is the
8-page discussion (checksum-proved identical to the copy already held). The primary edition
remains a **material request** — acquisition requires purchase or interlibrary loan of
Edel & Görg 2005 (ÄAT 50, Harrassowitz). This mirrors the Hoch 1994 situation for REQ-02: the
channel is only unblocked once the copyrighted edition is *locally owned and directly collated*.

The user-supplied Bělohoubková 2014 thesis materially **advances** REQ-01 (it reproduces the
apparatus and, per the plate audit, may reproduce primary facsimiles) but, being secondary,
does not by itself constitute direct collation of Edel & Görg 2005 — see
`REQ01_APPARATUS_DETERMINATION.md`.
