# Recruitment brief — two independent Linear B annotators

We seek **two independent specialists** to annotate administrative roles on a 160-item Linear B word-form
sample, to test the feasibility of a **blinded, structure-first** role-induction benchmark (the model never
sees phonetic readings; experts establish the ground-truth roles).

## Required expertise (each annotator)
- Mycenaean Greek;
- Linear B epigraphy;
- Mycenaean palace administration;
- Linear B corpus interpretation (Knossos + Pylos + Thebes material).

## Task
- ~160 items; independent annotation of coarse administrative role + gold tier + brief rationale/citation;
  estimated 4–8 hours.
- Two annotators work **independently** (no contact) to allow an inter-annotator agreement estimate.

## What we provide
- A per-item packet (reading, document context, neighbours, numerals/logograms, series/site/support/hand,
  source-edition citation) and a blank response template.
- The annotation instructions (`HUMAN_EXPERT_ANNOTATION_INSTRUCTIONS.md`).

## What we do NOT provide (by design)
- Any model prediction, "expected answer", Linear A data, or the other annotator's responses.
