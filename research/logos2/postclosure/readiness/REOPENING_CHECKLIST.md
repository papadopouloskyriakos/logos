# Reopening-event checklist (gatekeeping for any new campaign)

A reopening requires ALL of the following before any analysis touches the new material:

1. **Registered evidence event** — one of: licence-clean GORILA klasmatogram data; Anetaki II
   publication (also triggers the sealed-prediction scoring protocols); a distinct-lineage
   anchor spanning ≥4 slots; newly discovered inscriptions; materially improved epigraphic
   readings/imaging; independently identified archaeological/metrological/onomastic evidence.
   NOT sufficient: more compute, another optimizer, a broader language inventory, a new RAG
   model, threshold/weight changes, or disappointment with a negative.
2. **Intake record completed** (INTAKE_RECORD_TEMPLATE.md) with provenance, licence, hashes,
   and an as_of timestamp — BEFORE inspection beyond what identification requires.
3. **Contamination check** — does the item descend from the META_CONTINUITY_LA_eq_LB lineage?
   If yes, it cannot count as a distinct-lineage anchor (it may still add corpus mass).
4. **Seal protocol** — if the event is Anetaki II: score the 72h FRACTION_ORDER seal and the
   E213 predictions under their frozen decision rules FIRST, opening seals only per their
   manifest opening conditions; record outcomes append-only regardless of result.
5. **New preregistration** — a NEW campaign document (frozen before execution) or an
   append-only extension. LOGOS-2 conclusions are never rewritten; contradictions become
   ERRATUM records.
6. **Stop-list carry-over** — conditionally exhausted methods (final/METHOD_EXHAUSTION_UPDATE.md)
   stay barred unless the evidence event satisfies their named reopening condition.
