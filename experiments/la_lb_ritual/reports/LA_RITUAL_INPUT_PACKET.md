# LA_RITUAL_INPUT_PACKET — review packet A (LA-only)

Human-review packet for the LA ritual candidate inventory. **Contains no Linear B material and no
similarity information.** Machine-readable: `data/gold/la_ritual_candidate_packet.jsonl` (raw sign IDs,
documents, site, object type, chronology, ritual context, recurrence, independence flags, tier,
partition). The phonetic form is kept only in the gitignored internal manifest for reproduction; it is
NOT in this packet. Known ritual forms are quarantined to `KNOWN_PAIR_DEVELOPMENT` and are not part of
the confirmatory candidate universe.
