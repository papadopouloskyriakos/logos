## Executive summary

The campaign ran **105 pre-registered epochs** across **34 method families** (F1–F23 + campaign-wide), each with a
committed prediction, controls, an adaptive null, and a mechanical verdict computed from held-out corpus data — never
by the proposer LLM. It was designed to find a *real* Linear A reading and to refuse a *fitted* one.

It refused a fitted one. **Zero transfer licences were earned; the highest claim layer reached is L3 (functional-
structural); no L4+ (semantic / phonetic / language-ID / translation) claim is authorized.** The absolute-value gate
(E102) fails on every criterion because the campaign's apparent multi-method signal collapses, under a dependency-
adjusted independence audit, to a **single independent channel** (context co-occurrence).

What the campaign *did* produce is durable and honest:

- **One graduated relative finding — A-initial positional enrichment (E022/E023/E024/E103/E103R).** In Linear A
  administrative units of ≥2 signs, the sign **A** occupies initial position far above its within-unit multiset
  expectation. It survives a matched adaptive null (p=.0002, best-of-72 maxT, E022), cross-site held-out
  (significant in 9/10 sites + leave-one-site-out, E023), multi-axis held-out (5/6 support types + 4/4
  chronological phases, E024), and the closure adjudication under **three overlapping segmentation/selection
  variants derived from a single corpus lineage** (editor / divider-strict / numeral-anchored ledger-heads; A is
  rank-1 by best-of-universe maxT in every variant, z 16.6/16.8/7.5 — ~2.4× the next sign in the editor baseline
  (2.2× divider, 1.1× numeral-anchored); verdict class unchanged under the corrected
  categorical joint null, E103R). This is segmentation **robustness**, not independent replication. It is a
  **RELATIVE positional constraint (L2/L3), NOT a phonetic value** — compatible with a recurrent initial functional
  or morphological slot; prefixhood, productivity, sound, and meaning are not established.
- **A campaign-wide null certifying the discipline (E104 pilot → E104R certification):** running the *actual*
  graduation decision rule on structure-matched, signal-free nulls fabricates a graduated finding **1/400** times
  under the corrected categorical maxT (observed rate 0.25%; one-sided exact CP95 upper bound **1.18% ≤ 2%**,
  the committed bound-based criterion) — vs **121/400** with the best-of-universe maxT deflation ablated on the
  same nulls (suppression factor **121×**). Power is retained (planted 12% prefix recovered 40/40; this
  establishes power only for effects of that general magnitude). The original E104 run (1/200; CP95 upper 2.35%)
  is a **pilot** — a valid empirical calibration of the rule-as-implemented; certification language attaches to
  E104R's bound-based verdict `CAMPAIGN_NULL_GATES_CERTIFIED`. "End-to-end" means the graduation decision rule
  end-to-end (the positive control and LOMO are not re-executed per null); the absolute-value-gate zero is
  structural, by construction. A's z≈17 sits far outside the certified null band.
- **A method-diverse re-confirmation of underdetermination (Turing morphogenesis E091–E095 (+ E096 de-authorized)
  + cross-disciplinary E097–E102):** two whole disciplines of imported machinery (graph reaction-diffusion with
  mechanically-verified Turing instability; error-correcting codes / belief propagation; Potts spin-glass; causal
  source separation; topological persistence; global network-flow) calibrate on blinded Linear B but deliver
  **no Linear A decipherment**. The one genuine linguistic signal they surface (E099: the weak Linear B vowel
  structure is frequency-*invariant*, not a frequency artifact) is **single-channel and LA-untestable**.

## Blockers

- **Anchor independence (dominant):** only one independent linguistic channel exists in the accessible data. A
  second independent channel is **necessary but not sufficient** — value assignment additionally requires
  externally anchored evidence (bilinguals, secure multi-slot onomastics, metrological ties).
- **Source-blocked:** stroke/palaeographic contours (whose primary value is inventory/transcription, and which is
  the candidate 2nd channel) and the full Ariadne-2025 Anetaki edition are not accessibly available.
- **Not blockers:** corpus size (E093 shows LA is data-sufficient for the weak signal) and detector power (E104R
  certified; positive controls recover planted signal).

## Exact remaining uncertainty

Linear A words carry robust *relative/positional* structure (the A-initial slot, ledger KU-RO terminals, register
strata) but their **absolute sound values remain undetermined**: with one independent channel and no external
multi-slot anchor lattice, the value-assignment space is astronomically under-constrained (consistent with the
anchor-lattice `ANCHOR_LATTICE_UNDERDETERMINED` finding). No sign receives a licensed phonetic value.
