## Primary preregistered question

**Does the A-initial enrichment participate in a predictive paradigm, or is it merely positional
enrichment?** ("A" remains an anonymous sign; no value, no meaning.)

## Corpus interface (FROZEN with this prereg)

- Units: silver editor words, len ≥ 2 signs, from `inscriptions_structured.json` (sha256 in
  `data_manifest.sha256`); each word carries site, support, context (chronology), doc id.
- Primary split: site-stratified 80/20 fit/holdout at the WORD-TYPE level (types are assigned
  wholly to fit or holdout so held-out FORM prediction is genuine; seed
  sha256(master|E207|split)).
- Holdout axes for survival checks: leave-one-site-out (HT out = hardest), chronology strata
  (LMIB vs rest), support strata (Tablet vs rest), segmentation variant (editor words vs the
  B_divider_strict merge variant from E103's frozen scheme builder).
- Transcription-uncertainty stability: 5% random sign deletion, 20 replicates.

## Model battery (all evaluated by held-out per-sign log-loss with add-1 smoothing; model
complexity charged in bits: log2-cost of lexicon entries + parameters, dependency-deduplicated)

1. **M0 no-morphology:** sign unigram + word-length geometric.
2. **M1 independent-token:** per-slot-independent unigram (length-conditioned).
3. **M2 positional:** position-specific sign distributions (first/medial/final) — captures
   A-initial enrichment WITHOUT any morphology. **This is the null the question is about.**
4. **M3 stem+affix:** optional initial-affix slot (affix inventory induced from fit data, cap 8)
   + stem lexicon (PYP/MDL-induced); P(word)=P(affix)·P(stem).
5. **M4 clitic-compatible:** M3 with affix rate conditioned on administrative context proxy
   (entry-head vs not, from the E206 S1 definition).
6. **M5 subregular / probabilistic finite-state:** strictly-2-local sign model (bigram over
   position-classes) — doubles as the templatic comparison at the sign-class level.
7. **M6 adaptor-grammar (simplified, disclosed):** two-level PYP — PYP over words backing off to
   PYP over (optional A-slot + stem) parses; concentration params fixed (d=0.5, θ=1.0), NOT
   tuned (no dev set).

## Matched synthetic corpora (the decisive control)

200 synthetics preserving: token count, type count, sign unigram frequencies, word-length
distribution, **A-initial rate**, site and support distribution — generated by within-site
shuffling of non-initial signs across words (initial sign kept in place). Any paradigm signal
(M3/M6 advantage) must exceed the 95th percentile of the synthetic distribution (plus-one p);
synthetics by construction have the enrichment but no paradigm.

## Mechanical verdict vocabulary (only these; Holm over the M3-vs-M2 and M6-vs-M2 pair)

- **POSITIONAL_ONLY** — no stem/adaptor model beats M2 after complexity charge, or beats it but
  not the synthetic band.
- **PREDICTIVE_FUNCTIONAL_CLASS** — an A-conditioned model improves held-out prediction beyond
  M2 + synthetic band, but without recurring-stem structure.
- **CLITIC_COMPATIBLE** — M4 specifically carries the improvement (context-conditioned rate).
- **AFFIX_COMPATIBLE** — M3/M6 beat M2 after MDL charge + synthetic band + ≥10 recurring stems
  attested both with and without the A-slot in fit data, and the advantage survives ≥3/4
  holdout axes.
- **PARADIGMATIC_MORPHOLOGY_SUPPORTED** — AFFIX_COMPATIBLE + systematic alternants (≥5 stems
  attested with ≥2 distinct affix slots) + survives ALL holdout axes + transcription stability.

The words "prefix", "productive", and "morphological slot" are permitted ONLY if the final
category is reached (and then still carry no phonetic/semantic content).
