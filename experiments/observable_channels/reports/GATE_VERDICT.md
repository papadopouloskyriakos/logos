# OBSERVABLE_CHANNEL_READY — gate verdict

Mechanical verdict on the Observable Administrative Channel Recovery programme (Constitution v2.0;
`PROJECT_CHARTER.md` gate). Synthesizes Experiments 1–4. Append-only (Art. XVII).

## The central falsifiable question

> *Can opaque word forms predict independently-observable administrative channels when lexical identity,
> site shortcuts, and document-series shortcuts are controlled?*

**Answer: NO.** Verdict: **`REFUTED`** at the load-bearing layer (L3 functional word-context).

## What the programme delivered

1. **A rigorous, well-powered L3 negative** (the primary result). Across two dense, balanced channels
   (457 + 1,593 A12 units — not underpower), opaque word forms carry **no transferable functional signal**
   once document structure is controlled; they overfit and transfer negatively. This closes the
   word-form→function route as a bounded negative — consistent with the label-route `NO_POWER` and the
   Egyptian-anchor `TRIVIAL_RECOVERY`. *A negative verdict is a successful scientific result (Preamble).*
2. **A characterized L2 structural channel** (secondary, `SUPPORTED` at L2): documents are internally
   templated (0.508 vs 0.398 held-out) and exhibit sparse-but-exact accounting closure (100% masked-line
   reconstruction where present). This is real but is the **already-known L2 layer** (segmentation/structure
   in the paper), and it is exactly the site/series/layout *convenience* the gate requires removing — so it
   does **not** earn a functional licence.

## Licences (Art. XV)

- **FUNCTIONAL_TRANSFER_LICENSE:** `NOT_EARNED` — the L3 word-context question is `REFUTED`.
- **STRUCTURAL_TRANSFER_LICENSE:** `NOT_YET_EARNED` — genuine held-out L2 evidence exists (Exp 3–4
  `SUPPORTED`), but the gate is not cleared: cross-site invariance is single-direction (template strong at
  non-KN, closure strong at KN; neither is bidirectional) and the O2+D8 pseudo-script leg has not been run.
  A STRUCTURAL licence would require completing Exp 5 (two-directional invariance) + Exp 6 (O2+D8) and
  clearing both — worthwhile only if an anonymous L2 structural transfer to Linear A is the goal.

## Disposition

Programme `COMPLETE` on the functional question (`REFUTED`); `OPEN-if-pursued` for a strictly L2 structural
licence (Exp 5–6 remain). No Linear A output authorized (SEMANTIC+ `NOT_AUTHORIZED`; STRUCTURAL not earned).
