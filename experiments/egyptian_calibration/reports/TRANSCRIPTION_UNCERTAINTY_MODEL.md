# TRANSCRIPTION_UNCERTAINTY_MODEL — §IX (tiers specified; not exercised)

Bounded design-analysis tiers `LOW / CENTRAL / HIGH` are specified for: damaged-sign alternatives,
uncertain segmentation, word-boundary alternatives, composite/decomposition uncertainty, projected
Linear B value uncertainty, sign-identity ambiguity, source-edition disagreement. No precise
probabilities are invented — documented tiers / bounded ranges only. **Not exercised** (blocked upstream
at the calibration corpus). If the design verdict were to reverse across tiers, the design would be
reported as fragile; that check is deferred until a model exists.
